## Advantages of Split

1. **Faster publication:** Paper Ia can submit immediately (core discovery is solid)
2. **Length resolution:** Both papers fit journal limits (8K + 6K vs 25K words)
3. **Targeted audiences:**
   - Ia → Mathematical physics / quantum foundations
   - Ib → Computational chemistry / applications
4. **Citation impact:** Two papers > one long paper for metrics
5. **Risk management:** If Ia accepted but Ib needs more work, core discovery is published

## Disadvantages of Split

1. **Story fragmentation:** Readers need both papers for full picture
2. **Duplication:** Some lattice setup must appear in both papers
3. **Coordination:** Must cross-reference carefully ("see companion paper")
4. **Submission timing:** Should submit together or sequentially?
